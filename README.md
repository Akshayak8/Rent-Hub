# RentHub

RentHub is a comprehensive platform designed to simplify the rental experience. Whether you're searching for a cozy apartment, a high-quality sound system for your event, or the perfect book to read, RentHub has got you covered. Our platform brings together a wide range of rental services, including houses, rooms, electronics, vehicles, books, furniture, musical instruments, party supplies, and event spaces, all in one convenient location.

## Features:
- **Browse and Compare:** Easily browse through our extensive collection of rental listings and compare options side by side to find the perfect fit for your needs.
- **Book with Ease:** Our user-friendly interface makes booking rentals a breeze. With just a few clicks, you can secure your rental and make arrangements for pickup or delivery.
- **Responsive Design:** RentHub is built with responsive design principles, ensuring a seamless experience across all devices, from desktops to smartphones.
- **Customizable Search:** Use our advanced search filters to narrow down your options based on location, price, availability, and more.
- **Secure Transactions:** Rest assured that your transactions on RentHub are safe and secure. We prioritize the protection of your personal information and payment details.

## Technologies Used:
RentHub is built using modern web technologies, including:
- **HTML5:** For structuring the content of our web pages.
- **CSS3:** For styling and enhancing the visual appearance of our platform.
- **JavaScript:** For adding interactivity and dynamic functionality to our website.
- **Bootstrap:** For responsive layout and design components that ensure a consistent user experience across devices.
